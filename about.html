about me

I'm an integrated, multimedia artist with a specialization in live events, installations, interactive and digital visual media.

Logan Smith

Logan is a multimedia artist based in Austin, Texas. He started as an sound designer for theatrical productions but branched out into technical directing for television, editing for video, designing projections for theater, 3D environmental design, and much more.

He holds a M.F.A. in Design and Technology for live performance from the University of Texas with a specialization in Integrated Media. Logan is adaptable to new kinds of digital media and is always looking at what’s next in technology for his creative works.
